Our journey

Our love story

Where it began
Summer 2018
The First Meeting
A chance encounter at a coffee shop in Notting Hill. James spilled his oat latte. Emma laughed. The rest, as they say, is history.
Kew Gardens
October 2018
First Date
A long walk through Kew Gardens on a crisp autumn afternoon — funnily enough, now the venue for their wedding. It just felt right.
Home sweet home
March 2021
Moving In Together
They found a little flat in Chiswick with terrible plumbing and the most beautiful light. They painted every room themselves and loved every minute.
She said yes
December 2024
The Proposal
Under the Christmas lights in Covent Garden, James got down on one knee. Emma said yes before he'd even finished the sentence. She still teases him about it.
